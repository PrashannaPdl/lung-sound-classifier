User Guide

How to use this diagnostic system efficiently:

  1. Upload: Click "Browse" to select your .wav file.
  2. Process: Click "Diagnose" and wait for the AI analysis.
  3. Zoom & Pan: Hover over the graphs and use your mouse wheel to zoom in/out. Click and drag to pan across the timeline.

Note on Invalid Audio: The system automatically rejects non-respiratory sounds (music, speech, silence).
